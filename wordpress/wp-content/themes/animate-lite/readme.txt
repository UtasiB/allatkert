== Description ==
Animate Lite is a stylish and gracious, charming and visually stunning, elegant and refined, modern and responsive pet care WordPress theme. Its a flexible and very purposeful theme, dedicated to pets and animal lovers. It is specially developed to provide a very appealing space for animal care, pet care centers, pet stores and all other pet related business ventures. This multipurpose theme can also be used for pet adoption, dog training classes, pet sitters, online pet shops, pet hotels, veterinary clinics, safari and zoo sites, animal shelter charities, safari parks, wildlife charities and aquariums.
